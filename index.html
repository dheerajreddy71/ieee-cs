Welcome to the IEEE Student Branch

We are dedicated to fostering interest in electrical engineering, electronics, and computer science among students.

About Us

The IEEE CS Student Branch Chapter at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education was established on 26th July 2024.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore and develop their skills in the field of computer science and engineering, and to promote innovation, entrepreneurship, and community service.

Benefits of IEEE Membership

  • Access to cutting-edge technical information and resources
  • Networking opportunities with professionals and students from around the world
  • Participation in conferences, workshops, and competitions
  • Opportunities for leadership and professional development
  • Discounts on IEEE publications and events
